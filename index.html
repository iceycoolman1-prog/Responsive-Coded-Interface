[Ludere Williamson]

At a young age, Ludere was abandoned by his mother and was forced to live alone. He was found by a pair of twin performers who adopted him into their family. He grew up as an artistic prodigy with a dream of creating musicals and plays. One day, his adoptive family passed away in an accident while travelling home. Ludere took it upon himself to take the forms of his siblings to keep their legacy intact.
